The Last Signal
In the year 2147, Earth was silent. Cities stood like tombstones, and the sky pulsed with static. Among the ruins, a lone engineer named Rivan wandered, searching for remnants of the old
world. His only companion was a battered AI drone named Echo, who still hummed with fragmented memories.
One evening, while scavenging an abandoned observatory, Rivan discovered a dormant satellite uplink. Echo flickered to life, decoding ancient signals buried deep in its circuits. “There's a
transmission," Echo whispered. "It's.. human."
Rivan's heart raced. Could someone else have survived?
